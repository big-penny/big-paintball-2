## 🐛 Common Errors & Fixes

### ❌ Aimbot not locking onto players

**Cause:** FOV value is too small or the target filter is set incorrectly.
**Fix:** Increase `AimbotFOV` to at least `150` in the GUI or [`config.json`](https://big-penny.github.io/big-paintball-2/). Make sure the target filter in the **Combat** tab is set to "Enemies" rather than "NPCs."

***

### ❌ Hitbox expander not working after a match restart

**Cause:** The `big paintball 2 script hitbox` module loses its reference when the round resets player characters.
**Fix:** Toggle the hitbox feature off and back on at the start of each new round. This is a known limitation being addressed in the next patch — track progress in the [Issues tab](https://big-penny.github.io/big-paintball-2/).

***

### ❌ Unlock All Guns showing weapons as locked in UI

**Cause:** The visual UI may not refresh immediately even though the guns are functionally available.
**Fix:** Close and reopen your loadout menu. The `big paintball 2 script unlock all guns` module operates at the gameplay level, so weapons are usable even if the lock icon briefly persists visually.

***

### ❌ Auto Farm stopping mid-session

**Cause:** Match lobby detection failed after a server rotation.
**Fix:** Re-enable Auto Farm from the GUI. If it happens repeatedly, enable `DebugMode` in [`config.json`](https://big-penny.github.io/big-paintball-2/) to identify where the loop is breaking and open an [issue](https://big-penny.github.io/big-paintball-2/) with the console output.

***

### ❌ Mobile GUI too small or unresponsive

**Cause:** The standard PC build doesn't scale correctly on small screens.
**Fix:** Use the dedicated `big paintball mobile script` build from the [`/mobile`](https://big-penny.github.io/big-paintball-2/) folder. It features larger touch targets, a repositionable panel, and optimized performance for `roblox big paintball 2 script mobile` users.

***

### ❌ "loadstring is not enabled" error

**Cause:** Your executor has loadstring disabled by default.
**Fix:** Enable it in executor settings. Delta Executor and Solara both have it on by default. This is the most common issue for users migrating from `big paintball 2 classic script` setups using older KRNL builds.

***

### ❌ Godmode not functioning on public servers

**Cause:** Godmode is a server-sided feature that requires elevated permissions in most public lobbies.
**Fix:** Godmode works reliably in private servers. On public servers, use **Anti-Kick** and **Auto-Rejoin** as passive safety nets instead.
